What Credentials Should Legitimate Agencies Hold?

Credentials are the licenses, certifications, and registrations that verify an agency operates legally and competently.

A legitimate Florida digital marketing agency holds active state business registration, platform certifications, and carries professional liability insurance.

    • Florida business registration — verify at Florida Division of Corporations (Sunbiz)

    • Google Partner status — verify at Google Ads Partners

    • Meta Business Partner — verify at Meta Partner Directory

    • Professional liability insurance — $1M minimum recommended

    • FTC endorsement-guide compliance for influencer/review work (source: ftc.gov)

Florida Statute §501.204 (the Florida Deceptive and Unfair Trade Practices Act) applies to marketing claims made on behalf of Wildwood businesses — agencies that promise guaranteed rankings or fabricate reviews expose their clients to legal risk under this statute (source: leg.state.fl.us).
