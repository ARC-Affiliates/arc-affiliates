What Credentials Should a Legitimate Agency Have?

Credentials are verifiable third-party certifications that prove platform competence. Require Google Partner, Meta Business Partner, and a registered Florida business entity before you sign.

    • Florida business registration — verify at Sunbiz.org (Florida Division of Corporations).

    • Google Partner status — public directory at google.com/partners.

    • Meta Business Partner — searchable at Meta's partner directory.

    • General liability insurance — minimum $1M recommended for contracts over $5,000.

    • Florida sales tax compliance — services are generally exempt, but digital goods may trigger tax under Florida Statute 212.05.

The typical onboarding process

    • Step 1: Discovery call — 30–60 minutes reviewing goals, competitors, and budget.

    • Step 2: Audit — the agency reviews your site, GBP, ads, and analytics over 5–10 business days.

    • Step 3: Strategy proposal — a written plan with KPIs, deliverables, and 2026 pricing.

    • Step 4: Kickoff & access — you grant admin access to Google Ads, Analytics, and Meta.

    • Step 5: Execution — campaigns launch within 2–3 weeks of kickoff.

    • Step 6: Reporting — monthly dashboards and a quarterly strategy review.

Vetting checklist before you hire

    • Confirm Florida business registration on Sunbiz.

    • Verify Google Partner and Meta Business Partner badges independently.

    • Request three references from clients in your industry.

    • Ask for sample monthly reports — not sales decks.

    • Confirm you will own all ad accounts, domains, and creative assets.

    • Get the cancellation policy in writing (30-day notice is standard).

    • Compare at least two proposals before signing.
