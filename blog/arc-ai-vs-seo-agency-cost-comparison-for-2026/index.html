What Credentials Should a Legitimate SEO Provider Have?

Before paying any SEO agency or platform, verify these credentials:

  • Registered business entity in good standing (check your state's Secretary of State database).
  • Compliance with FTC endorsement and advertising rules — ftc.gov.
  • Published privacy policy meeting state requirements (CCPA, CPRA, etc.).
  • Google Partner status or verifiable case studies with named clients.
  • Transparent reporting using third-party tools (Google Search Console, Analytics 4).
