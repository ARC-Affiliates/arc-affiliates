How Do I Verify an SEO Agency's Credentials in Florida?

Verifying an SEO agency's credentials means confirming business registration, platform certifications, and client-verifiable case studies before signing.

Check Florida's Sunbiz registry, Google Partner directory, and request three referenceable clients you can call directly.

What credentials to verify

  • Florida business registration: Search the agency on Sunbiz.org — Florida's official corporate registry.
  • Google Partner status: Check the Google Partners directory for verified Ads competency.
  • Local sales tax registration: Florida requires registration with the Florida Department of Revenue (Statute 212.18).
  • BBB profile: Look up the firm at bbb.org for complaint history.
  • General liability insurance: Reputable agencies carry $1M minimum coverage.

SEO agency vetting checklist

  1. Search the agency name + "reviews" on Google, Clutch, and BBB.
  2. Request three client references with phone numbers — and call them.
  3. Ask for two case studies with before/after Google Analytics screenshots.
  4. Confirm month-to-month or 90-day exit clause in the contract.
  5. Verify reporting cadence (monthly minimum) and what metrics are included.
  6. Ask who specifically will work on your account — in-house or outsourced.
  7. Confirm Florida business registration on Sunbiz.
  8. Get the proposal in writing with deliverables and timelines.
